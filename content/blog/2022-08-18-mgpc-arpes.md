Angle-resolved photoemission measurements on magnesium phthalocyanine (MgPc) films were performed using the **Australian Synchrotron** with a **toroidal analyzer**, achieving approximately **20 meV** effective bandwidth resolution in the conditions reported—sufficient to resolve low-energy molecular valence features relevant to hybridization at interfaces.

**DOI:** [10.1039/D2NA00385F](https://doi.org/10.1039/D2NA00385F)
