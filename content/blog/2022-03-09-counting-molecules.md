We present a Python-based pipeline for automated enumeration and categorization of molecules in STM images, demonstrated on **9-azidophenanthrene** species on surfaces. **Zernike moments** provide rotation-invariant descriptors for classification. The approach is packaged as a reusable **Python module** for microscopy image stacks.

**DOI:** [10.1016/j.simpa.2022.100301](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.simpa.2022.100301)
