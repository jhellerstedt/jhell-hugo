On Au surfaces, **DCA–Au–DCA** motifs can assemble into striped architectures. STM shows how local bonding and **C–H bond scission** participate in stabilizing the striped phase, tying molecular-scale chemistry to mesoscale patterns published in *JACS*.

**DOI:** [10.1021/jacs.2c10154](https://doi.org/10.1021/jacs.2c10154)
