We study chiral metal-organic frameworks assembled on Ag(111) using HAT-based ligands. Scanning tunneling microscopy and spectroscopy reveal spatially modulated electronic structure with **~80 meV** gaps tied to the chiral network. Tight-binding and **EPWE (extended plane-wave expansion)** calculations help connect the observed contrast and gap features to the underlying MOF electronic structure.

**DOI:** [10.1002/sstr.202500422](https://doi.org/10.1002/sstr.202500422)
