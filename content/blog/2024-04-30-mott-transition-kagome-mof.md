We investigate a **Cu–DCA kagome metal-organic framework** grown on hBN/Cu(111). The combined substrate and kagome geometry drive a rich phase diagram; spectroscopy points toward a **Mott-like correlated regime** in the appropriate filling/limit, reported in *Nature Communications*.

**DOI:** [10.1038/s41467-024-47766-8](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-47766-8)
